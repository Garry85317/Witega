About

About Witega

Witega Technology Development Co., Ltd. was established in Taichung in 2020 with the aim of enhancing the work efficiency and environmental hygiene safety of the domestic pig farming industry.
We provide livestock industry equipment and products, both domestically and internationally, and cooperate with government subsidies for the introduction of labor-saving automation equipment.
They specialize in producing labor-saving equipment suitable for the pig farming industry in Taiwan.

  • Offers wireless intelligent ultrasound devices and provides training and services related to pregnancy detection.
  • Specialize in customizing piglet work carts, carcass handling carts, and boar testing carts, among other labor-saving equipment.
  • Customized biosecurity cabins to achieve the biosecurity goals of the Taiwanese livestock industry.

Witega is dedicated to manufacturing equipment that meets the requirements of the Taiwanese farming environment.
We strive to surpass the quality of foreign manufacturers and provide fast warranty and repair services in Taiwan, ensuring that customers can make their purchases with confidence and use the equipment with peace of mind.
